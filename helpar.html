Project Description:

Identify 1 paper product out of 12 common types of paper product from the Paper Plus framework, and use the AR technology to improve its performance in terms of the 12 specified dimensions.

Empathize

We brainstormed several existing paper product that could benefit by AR technology. Ultimately we choosed the education sector to simplify the process to visualize 3D diagrams.

Interpret

From the response that we received by interviewing some university students, we identified that it is mainly the spatiality, scalability and flexibility that AR extends existing paper products the most. I.e. we can easily scale and view 3D items in AR in its actual size whereas such information projected on paper loses scale.

Ideate

We proposed 4 differnet solution to the target problem (with and without AR), and identified the core need by users later through a series of interviews.

Verify

From the responses that we received in speed Dating, our prototype could be improved by allowing more user interation methods within the App. Also, the size constraint of smart phone's screen limits the detail of content. It seems necessary to allow projection of the AR 3D model from the smart phone to a larger display.

Personal Contribution

I participated in the prototyping process with coding of the 3D object manipulation in Unity3D with Vuforia, filming the prototype demonstration, as well as the brainstorming process.
